Database Structure

An overview of the database architecture, highlighting how data is securely stored, accessed, and managed within the HMS.

  • Patient Table: Contains records like patient ID, name, medical history, and current treatments.
  • Appointment Table: Manages appointment details including time, doctor, and patient ID.
  • Medical Records Table: Stores comprehensive medical records, linked to patient IDs.
